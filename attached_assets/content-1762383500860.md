## About Elyria

### Our mission with Elyria Mail

To provide a secure, private email service that respects your right to communicate freely and securely, without compromising your personal data or privacy. Elyria Mail is not merely an email service; it is a declaration of digital independence.

#### E2E Encryption

Full encryption support for maximum privacy protection. Your emails are encrypted before leaving your device.

#### Zero-Knowledge

We cannot read, access, or decrypt your emails - ever. Your data remains completely private.

#### No Logging

Zero logging of IP addresses, metadata, or user activity. Your digital footprint disappears.

#### Security Audits

Regular security assessments and continuous monitoring to maintain the highest standards.
